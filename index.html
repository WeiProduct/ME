About

Builder first.

I'm Wei Fu. I run WeiProduct — a one-person AI consumer product studio. My philosophy is to ship narrow, design for repeated moments, and compound the apps that find their users.

Before this I studied Computer Science and Managerial Economics at UMass Amherst (BS, 2025). The combination is uncommon and it shapes how I prioritize: every feature is a hypothesis about user value; every release is a small bet.

I work primarily in Swift and SwiftUI on iOS, Python for tooling, and TypeScript on the web. I lean on the major model providers (OpenAI, Anthropic) rather than training from scratch — the value lives in product surfaces, not the weights.

  • Build

    Swift / SwiftUI, TypeScript, Python, Node.js, Vercel, App Store Connect.

  • AI

    OpenAI, Anthropic, Whisper, Realtime APIs, prompt engineering, evals.

  • Business

    Pricing, ASO, retention loops, financial modeling, growth experiments.
